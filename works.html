1. DownForce

As an avid reader of Hacker News, I often found myself missing out on top stories or not having the time to read through entire blogs. To solve this, I decided to develop a mobile app using React Native and Swift for my personal use. Although the app is quite simple, it features two key functionalities:

Interactive Dynamic Widget for iOS: Every 60 minutes, the top story from Hacker News is displayed on my widget, with an option to bookmark it for later reading.

On-Device Blog Summarization using LLM: To quickly grasp the content of a blog, I implemented a summarization feature that automatically extracts a concise summary using Generative AI and Large Language Model (LLM) techniques.

Note: I named this app after my love with Formulae One
